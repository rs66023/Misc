About Fantastiqo

The story behind the startup
Origin

Fantastiqo is a company incorporated in the year 2020 and it is an emerging startup launched in the period of corona pandemic and is becoming more famous and famous due to its uniqueness and creativity

Passion

Every team member is very passionate about their work and we would be spreading our working areas to many more fields such as YouTube Marketing, Blogging, Content Writings and many more for the current and the upcoming members of the Fantastiqo.

Design

The basic design of this startup is based on the area of working with Social Media Marketing and Graphic Designing and then it expertised interns in these respective fields to make them professionals.

Support

Fantastiqo is evolving in its own ways and the pillar support of the Fantastiqo are Ishika Narnolia, Sakshi Gupta, Preeti Kumari and Aman. Fantastiqo core members is a group of talented experts dedicated and committed to offer untarnished and spotless services

The Founder

Shubham Choudhary

A young entrepreneur launches his own startup at the age of 25.

The idea behind this start-up was to involve students in something productive when Corona was at Peak and Life was at risk. So he believed why not to use this time and make students professional into areas Such as- Social media marketing, Graphic desingning and various other domains.

Founder and CEO of Fantastiqo.

I belong to Patna and I believe that every person is talented and amazing in itself. Just a right path is needed for them so that their creativity could be turned into their skills.

Cheers to all of you for your efforts which you all have made in. Fantastiqo really want to thank all its amazing members for their huge love and support.
